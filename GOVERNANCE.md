# Governance & Compliance

DigitalServerHost (DSH) maintains strict security and governance standards to protect our enterprise infrastructure and SaaS solutions.

## Organization Certification

DigitalServerHost is an **Org-Certified** entity. This status is verified via DNS on the `digitalserverhost.com` domain.

## Access Control & Security

To ensure the integrity of our repositories and infrastructure:

- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Mandatory for all members and outside collaborators.
- **Identity Verification**: Members must use a verified email address from the `@digitalserverhost.com` domain.
- **Verification Keys**: Official public GPG keys for core members are maintained in the [.github/keys/](.github/keys/) directory for manual commit verification.

## Member Requirements

All prospective members must fulfill the following criteria before joining the organization:

1. Enable 2FA on their GitHub account.
2. Link and verify a professional email address (e.g., `user@digitalserverhost.com`).
3. Adhere to the terms outlined in our `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`.

## Governance Inquiries

For questions regarding governance or compliance, please contact the DSH Security Team at **support@digitalserverhost.com**.
